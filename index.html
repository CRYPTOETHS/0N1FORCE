Storytelling

0N1 is an anime media and technology company, building a full suite of multimedia products centered around the 0N1 Force intellectual properties, including comic books, digital collectibles, apparel, video games, augmented reality, live experiences, and more. Our first venture is in comics: For months, a group of passionate community members have worked alongside Eisner award-winning author Paul Jenkins to develop the backstory, character, and world of 0N1 Hunter X. This is the first of many community-led initiatives and 0n1 is committed to continuing to unlock our community’s creativity through partnering them with industry leading talent.
